# Qualified Legal Review Checklist

- Company legal name, registered address, trade licence and tax/VAT details
- Customer/support contact details and escalation process
- Privacy collection, purpose, storage, processors, access/deletion and breach handling
- Cookies, analytics and marketing consent
- SSLCommerz/payment processing, failed payments, refund method and timing
- Return eligibility, exclusions, inspection, replacement and damaged-product handling
- Delivery estimates, COD/remote-area limits, failed delivery and liability
- Product safety, age guidance, warranty and children’s-product disclaimers
- Accounts, authentication, uploaded evidence, review moderation and user-generated content
- Notification preferences, email/SMS/WhatsApp processing
- File, order, audit, support and dispute retention/legal holds
- Cloudinary, courier, email, analytics and monitoring subprocessors
- Governing law, Bangladesh consumer requirements and dispute resolution

Record metadata in `/admin/legal-review`. “REVIEWED” must name a qualified reviewer and date; it does not cause the application to claim legal approval automatically.
